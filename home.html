Among the BSIT 2nd year students, there is a diverse range of backgrounds and experiences, each contributing to the unique learning environment of the program. The students are exposed to a comprehensive curriculum, which includes courses in programming, database management, web development, and network administration, among others.

Despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, the BSIT 2nd year students have shown remarkable resilience and adaptability in continuing their studies remotely. With the guidance of their dedicated professors and the support of the EVSU community, these students are well on their way to becoming the next generation of IT leaders and innovators.

About EVSU-CC

EVSU- CC is home to some of the brightest minds in the field of Information Technology, particularly among its 2nd year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students. These students are equipped with the foundational knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the fast-paced and ever-evolving world of IT.
